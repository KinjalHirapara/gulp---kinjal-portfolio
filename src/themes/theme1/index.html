Features

The best of Silicon Valley living is at your fingertips within this impeccably remodeled home. Nestled in a low-key setting just moments from shopping, dining, schools, and work, the residence affords effortless, modern living with a stylish twist.

Entertaining has never been easier thanks to the home’s ingenious redesign. Open, flexible gathering spaces and a knock-out chef’s kitchen create warmth, elegance, and efficiency. The private pool retreat offers an outdoor kitchen, overlooked by an expansive upper-level deck perfect for parties.

Also showcased are gorgeously appointed bedrooms, a plethora of designer fixtures, and high-tech details for easy connectivity. In addition, you can quickly reach nearby schools, walk to cafes and outdoor recreation, or bike to shopping, medical centers, and neighboring employers like Apple. Prepare to be swept off your feet by this beauty!

THE HOME

  • Boasting a thorough and meticulous redesign, this breathtaking home grants an unparalleled combination of accessibility, versatility, and style
  • Extensive crown molding, beamed ceiling treatments, and upgrades that include fashionable flooring, designer fixtures, hanging doors, and chic paint colors deliver high-impact style
  • Lighting by Legrand and Lutron, Nest climate control, and Wink connectivity add cutting-edge convenience
  • Free-flowing, flexibly designed living, dining, and family areas adjoin the island kitchen, excellent for entertaining
  • Jaw-dropping kitchen features include glamorous quartz countertops with waterfall edges, custom soft-close cabinetry, a wine cooler, and high-end amenities like Jenn-Air refrigeration and a Thermador oven range with pot-filler
  • Well-appointed bedrooms include a versatile home office with private entrance, junior master suite with dual showers, and sprawling master retreat displaying a sitting room, his-and-her closets, and dreamy bathroom with rainfall shower and freestanding tub
  • French doors open to a private swimming pool and outdoor kitchen surrounded by new, low-maintenance landscaping, while an expansive upper-level deck is ideal for outdoor gatherings overlooking the pool

THE LOCATION

  • This home’s low-key but highly desirable setting is just moments from shopping, dining, schools, and work
  • Walk or quickly bike to Laurelwood Elementary and Peterson Middle, and enjoy easy access to Wilcox High (buyer to verify)
